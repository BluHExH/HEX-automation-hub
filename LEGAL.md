When using this tool to interact with websites or services, you must:

1. **Respect robots.txt**: Check and comply with the website's robots.txt file
2. **Follow Terms of Service**: Adhere to the terms of service of any website or service you interact with
3. **Rate Limiting**: Use appropriate delays between requests to avoid overloading servers
4. **Data Usage**: Only collect and use data in accordance with applicable privacy laws and regulations
